Kumarakom Village

Kumarakom is a picturesque village situated on the banks of Vembanad Lake in Kerala's Kottayam district. It is famous for its backwaters, lush greenery, and traditional Kerala culture.

Visitors to Kumarakom can experience Kerala's unique culture by watching traditional Kathakali and Kalaripayattu performances, exploring the serene backwaters on houseboats, and enjoying local cuisine.
