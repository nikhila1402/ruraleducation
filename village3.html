Aranmula Village

Aranmula is a village in Pathanamthitta district known for its unique cultural and religious traditions. It is famous for the Aranmula Parthasarathy Temple, where an annual boat race called the "Aranmula Boat Race" takes place during the Onam festival.

The village is also renowned for its metal mirrors known as "Aranmula Kannadi," which are handmade using a secret alloy and craftsmanship passed down through generations.
